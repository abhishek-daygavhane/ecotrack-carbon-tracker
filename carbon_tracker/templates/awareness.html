⏳ Climate Crisis Timeline

1850s
Industrial Revolution begins — Coal-powered factories start releasing CO₂ at unprecedented scale. Atmospheric CO₂ was 280 ppm.
1958
Keeling Curve starts — First systematic CO₂ measurement begins. Reading: 315 ppm. Scientists start tracking the rise.
1988
IPCC established — Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change formed. Global scientific consensus on human-caused warming begins.
1997
Kyoto Protocol — First international treaty to reduce greenhouse gas emissions. India signed as a developing nation.
2015
Paris Agreement — 196 countries commit to keeping warming below 1.5–2°C. India pledged 40% renewable energy by 2030.
2023
Hottest year on record — Global average temperature hit 1.48°C above pre-industrial levels. Record wildfires, floods worldwide.
2030
Critical deadline — Scientists say emissions must fall 43% by 2030 to stay below 1.5°C. Every action today counts!

💪 What YOU Can Do Right Now

🌱 As an Individual
  • Track and reduce your daily carbon footprint
  • Plant trees — native species like Neem, Peepal
  • Switch to renewable energy where possible
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle every day
  • Choose a plant-rich diet even partially
👨‍👩‍👧 As a Community
  • Join or start a local environment group
  • Organise tree plantation drives
  • Promote carpooling in your neighbourhood
  • Advocate for cycle lanes and public transport
  • Educate children about climate change
🏛️ At Policy Level
  • Vote for leaders with strong climate policies
  • Support India's 500 GW solar target
  • Advocate for EV subsidies and infrastructure
  • Support carbon pricing and clean energy laws
  • Hold companies accountable for emissions
